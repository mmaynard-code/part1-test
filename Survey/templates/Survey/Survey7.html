Section 1

In the following task you are matched with one of the other participants from the previous task. The amount you earn in this task will count towards your payment that you are going to receive at the end of the experiment.

You have received 20 Monetary Units (MUs). You can give some of it to this other participant (please round up to one decimal, for instance 0.6). The other participant has also received 20 MU and can also divide this amount according to his/her will between him/her and you.

Your reward in this task depends on how much you give the other participant, and how much they give you: Your share is calculated as the sum of what you kept, and what you received from the other participant.

  • If your share of what you kept, and what you received from the other participant, is more than their share of the sum of what you gave, and what they kept then you will have won this task and you will receive the amount that you choose to keep yourself as payment at the end of the experiment.
  • If your share is lower, then you receive the amount that the other participant chose to give to you.
  • If your share and the other participants shares are equal, there will be a lottery between the two of you in which there is 50% chance that you win and receive the money that you kept, and a 50% chance that you lose and receive nothing.

    • Now please make a decision how much of your 20 Monetary Units (MUs) would like to give to the other participant.
