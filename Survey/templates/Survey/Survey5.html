Life during the pandemic makes online meetings necessary. Having your camera and microphone off during online meetings, however, allows you to do other things in the meantime, paying only half attention to the meeting. The MORE people turn the camera and microphone off during a meeting, the lower the productivity of the firm is, hence there will be LOWER bonuses at the end of the year.

Imagine that you are in such a situation together with 3 others. Your monetary rewards depend on your choice and of the choice of others. They are displayed in the following table.
